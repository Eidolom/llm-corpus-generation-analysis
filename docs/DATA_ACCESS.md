# TEC Corpus Access (By Request)

The TEC corpus is private and cannot be published in this repository.

## How to Request Access

Email the maintainer with:

- Your full name and affiliation
- The purpose of use (research/teaching)
- Confirmation you will not redistribute the corpus

Requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Approved users will receive a private download link.

## Notes

- The corpus is shared only with approved users.
- Please do not commit the TEC corpus into any public repository.
